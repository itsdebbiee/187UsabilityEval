No Breadcrumbs

This site doesn’t have breadcrumbs. This gives the user more responsibility to remember his/her actions and the information on previous pages. There are no indications of where the user is besides the browser address or perhaps the homepage of the respective site. Without breadcrumbs this site is harder to navigate and things are not easily retrievable.

Logo changes

Logo - The logo changes when user navigates throughout the site and the user cannot return back to the homepage from most of the site. The university’s brand gets lost and mixed up. We do not know if it’s primarily a research school, an athletic school or just a clustered school.
