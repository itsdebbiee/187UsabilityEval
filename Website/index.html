Usability Evaluation

Using Neilson's 10 Heuristics to critique and analyze Georgia State University's website

Analyzed site: http://www.gsu.edu/

THIS DOES NOTHING

    Usability Evaluation
  • Home
  • Visibility of system status
  • Match between system & the real world
  • User Control & Freedom
  • Consistency & Standards
  • Error Prevention
  • Recognition rather than recall
  • Flexibility & efficiency of use
  • Aesthetic & Minimalist Design
  • Help users recognize, diagnose, & recover from errors
  • Help & documentation
  • Ninja Pandas

What is the point of this?

Our objective is to understand what kinds of things websites should do in order to have a good user experience. By analyzing and seeing what this website has done, we get a clearer idea on what to do and what not to do.

This analysis has been done by Michelle Wang, Debbie Pan and Harry Ball Sack.
