What is the point of this?

Our group, Harry, Debbie and Michelle conducted a usability evaluation on Georgia State University using Nielsen’s Ten Heuristics for User Interface Design. We analyzed the user-friendly aspects of the website as well as features that needed improvement. Our objective is to understand what kinds of things websites should do in order to have a good user experience. By analyzing and seeing what this website has done, we get a clearer idea on what to do and what not to do.
