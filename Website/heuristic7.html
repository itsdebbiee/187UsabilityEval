No Visa Payment Option

Panther Pay only allows MasterCard, Discover, and American Express credit cards for student account payments- not Visa. This is very inconvenient for students or payers, since Visa Cards are very common in our daily lifestyle.

Efficiency

On the home page of the GSU, it is time-efficient for us to see a little blurb of what the article is about before clicking into it. This way, the user can decide, while remaining on the home page, if he or she wants to read that article. If not, then they can continue scrolling through the home page and find something else. This saves time for the user and is also simply adds a nice interactive design aspect to the website.
